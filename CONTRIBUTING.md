## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions to improve this open-source protocol! Whether you're fixing a typo, clarifying a step, or adding data from a new experiment, your input is valuable.

## Reporting Issues

If you find a mistake, ambiguity, a safety concern, or if you're having trouble reproducing the results, please [open an issue](https://github.com/YourUsername/YourRepoName/issues) (replace with your repo link).

Please be as detailed as possible, including:

*   What you expected to happen.
*   What actually happened.
*   The exact materials, equipment, and parameters you used.

## Suggesting Improvements (Pull Requests)

This is the preferred way to make changes.

1.  **Fork** the repository to your own GitHub account.
2.  **Create a new branch** for your changes (e.g., `git checkout -b feature/improve-curing-step`).
3.  **Make your changes** and **commit** them with clear messages.
4.  **Push** your branch to your fork on GitHub.
5.  **Open a Pull Request** from your branch to this repository's main branch.
6.  In your Pull Request, describe *what* you changed and *why*. If you optimized a step, please include any data or images that support your improvement (e.g., photos, contact angle measurements, microscope images).
